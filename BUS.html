BUS


The BUS of the computer is the wires that connects everything together, mainly connects things to the motherboard. The BUS transfers the information from many parts to the head of the computer. The BUS tells the computer what is happening so that it can function. These wires keep the computer together, because without it, it would not work. The wires vary from size, they power so many different parts of the computer. Each wire is made specially for carrying out certain functions.

History

  • In computers the BUS were very big, and bulky. The BUS has become faster, and faster. It does a great job on making the processing speeds fasters. The bus is a lot smaller these days, because people have found better materials, and greater designs. These days the BUS is very important to keep the computer working.
