Keyboard


The keyboard is the part of the computer
that allows people to type things into the computer.
The keyboard has letters, and many other pieces.
This allows people to tell the computer what to do. It acts as a control panel. The keyboard is the only way to use the computer, and tell it what to do.

History

    Before keyboards came out, people used what is know as a typewriter. The typewriter had keys like the keyboards have today. The keys pressed are printed on a piece of paper, just like a printer. The typewriter was made of many gears, and it was very bulky. These days keyboards consist of electronics. The keyboards are cheap to make, and they are very slim. The keyboard puts the letters on the computer. There are so many types of keyboards.
